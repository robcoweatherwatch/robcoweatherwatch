RobCo Weather Watch

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RobCo Weather Watch ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you interact with our Facebook Page (facebook.com/robcoweatherwatch) and Instagram account (instagram.com/robcoweatherwatch).

1. Information We Collect

We do not independently collect personal information from users. Any data associated with interactions on our social media pages (such as likes, comments, or messages) is subject to Meta's data practices. We may receive messages or comments you voluntarily send us.

2. How We Use Information

We use information you share with us (such as direct messages) only to respond to your inquiries. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not operate a website that uses tracking cookies or analytics
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect email addresses unless you voluntarily provide them

4. Meta Platforms

Our presence on Facebook and Instagram is governed by Meta's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We use the Meta Graph API to publish weather content to our own pages. We do not use the API to access, store, or process data about other users. Please review Meta's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our weather page:

  • Meta Graph API — to publish photos and updates to our Facebook Page and Instagram account
  • WeatherFlow Tempest — to retrieve weather station data for forecasts
  • National Weather Service (weather.gov) — to retrieve public forecast data

6. Children's Privacy

Our weather content is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any information shared with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: facebook.com/robcoweatherwatch
