Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

RobCo Weather Watch ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Facebook Page facebook.com/robcoweatherwatch and the Instagram account instagram.com/robcoweatherwatch. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our social media presence and any tools or applications we use to manage and publish content.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors to our social media pages beyond what is provided voluntarily through comments, messages, or reactions. We do not operate a website that collects user data, run advertisements, or sell any products or services.

How We Use Information

Any information shared with us through direct messages or comments is used solely to respond to inquiries or provide weather-related information to our community. We do not share, sell, or distribute personal information to third parties.

Meta Platforms (Facebook & Instagram)

Our pages are hosted on Meta's platforms. Meta may collect data about visitors and followers in accordance with their own policies. We encourage you to review Meta's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle user data.

We use the Meta Graph API solely to publish weather forecast graphics and updates to our own Facebook Page and Instagram account. We do not use the API to collect, store, or process any user data from followers or visitors.

Content Publishing Tool

We use a browser-based internal tool to create and publish weather graphics. This tool:

  • Runs entirely in the browser — no data is sent to any server we operate
  • Stores only a Page Access Token locally in the browser (localStorage) on the device used by the page administrator
  • Does not collect, transmit, or store any visitor or follower data
  • Communicates only with Meta's Graph API endpoints to publish content to our own page

Cookies

We do not operate a standalone website that sets cookies. Any cookies associated with our Facebook or Instagram pages are set by Meta and governed by their policies.

Children's Privacy

Our weather content is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us by messaging our Facebook Page at facebook.com/robcoweatherwatch.
